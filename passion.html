My Passion - Photography

A winding road where whispers meet,

Green arches rise, a shaded street,

Nature’s hands make journeys sweet.

A little shadow, a patch of snow,

Eyes like lanterns, softly aglow,

Grace in silence, wherever they go,

Sitting content by the warm light’s glow.

A white bloom gentle, pure and bright,

Framed by green in soft daylight,

A quiet painting, calm and right.

In the dark night, a beacon in sight,

The ice cream shop glows gentle and bright,

Calling hearts with its sweetest light.

The birds sitting on the power lines,

In the sky so peaceful, calm designs,

A quiet song the daylight aligns.

The misty, fog-filled sky in a chill embrace,

Whispers of cold weave through time and space,

A quiet world, serene and full of grace.

The evening sky, a dance of red and blue waves,

Colors blending softly as daylight caves,

A tranquil painting that the horizon paves.

The railway station platform, alive with dreams,

Hopes fluttering softly like distant streams,

Every step a story, or so it seems.
